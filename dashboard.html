📖 Signal Guide
Setup Signals
Trend Pullback
A stock in a confirmed uptrend has pulled back to key support (20/50-day MA) on declining volume. You're buying a temporary dip in a stock that's already proven it wants to go higher.
Triggers when: Price above 50-day MA + within 5–7% of MA support + RSI 25–55 + volume declining on pullback.
Watch for: A reversal candle at support with volume picking back up.
Volatility Breakout
Price has been coiling in a tight range (compression) near highs. Like a spring — the tighter the coil, the bigger the potential move when it breaks.
Triggers when: Bollinger Band width in bottom 30% of recent history + price within 8% of 52-week high + strong technical score.
Watch for: Close above resistance on 2x+ average volume.
Short Squeeze
Large % of float is sold short. If price moves up, short sellers must buy to cover, accelerating the move. Can cause explosive 20–50%+ runs.
Triggers when: Short float >15% + days-to-cover >3 + price turning up + volume accelerating.
Watch for: Sustained volume, shorts unable to push lower. Size carefully — can reverse violently.
Earnings Drift
Post-Earnings Announcement Drift (PEAD) — the market under-reacts to earnings beats. Stock drifts higher for weeks as more investors pile in.
Triggers when: Quarterly earnings growth >15% + RSI below 65 + both tech and fundamental scores strong.
Watch for: Institutional buying in 2–4 weeks after the beat.
Score Definitions (0–1)
Upside Score
Master score combining all six factor groups. Above 0.65 = high conviction. Bot only alerts when this clears threshold AND all other gates pass.
Risk Score
How dangerous to hold (lower = safer). Combines liquidity, ATR volatility, gap history, price level. Bot filters out anything above 0.60.
Tech Score
Trend (price vs MAs), volatility compression, RSI, volume accumulation on up-days vs down-days.
Fund Score
Revenue/earnings growth, margin trends, valuation vs sector peers (PEG, EV/Sales), balance sheet health.
Sent Score
News sentiment, analyst upgrades/downgrades, price target changes, unusual options flow.
Struct Score
Short interest (% float + days to cover), float size, institutional ownership, insider buying.
Market Regime Labels
■ Risk On Strong — SPY trending up, breadth healthy, low VIX. Best for momentum.
■ Risk On — Generally positive. Most setups work.
■ Choppy — Mixed. Stick to highest conviction, reduce size.
■ Risk Off — Market under pressure. Defensive posture.
■ Panic — Broad selloff. Cash is a position.
Options Strategies
Long Call / Long Put
Pay premium upfront. Stock must move enough to profit. Best when IV rank <30 (options are cheap). Long call = bullish. Long put = bearish. Max loss = what you paid.
Debit Spreads
Buy one option, sell another at a further strike. Cheaper than naked option. Capped max profit but lower breakeven. Best when IV is elevated.
Cash-Secured Put
Sell a put below price. Collect premium immediately. Keep all if stock stays above strike. Bullish/neutral. Need cash to buy shares if assigned.
Credit Spreads
Sell one option, buy protection further OTM. Collect net premium. Profit if stock stays away from short strike. Defined risk. Best in high-IV environments.
Iron Condor
Sell put spread + call spread simultaneously. Collect premium on both sides. Win if stock stays in range by expiry. Best in choppy/high-IV markets. Fully defined risk.
